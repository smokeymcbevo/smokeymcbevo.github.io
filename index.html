About the Longhorns

The Texas Longhorns football program is one of the most storied in college football history. Based in Austin, Texas, the Longhorns compete in the SEC and play their home games at Darrell K Royal–Texas Memorial Stadium — one of the largest stadiums in the country.

The Stadium

Darrell K Royal–Texas Memorial Stadium has been home to Longhorns football since 1924. With a capacity of over 100,000 fans, game days in Austin are an unforgettable experience. The roar of the crowd and the burnt orange sea of fans make it one of college football's most electric atmospheres.

Arch Manning

Arch Manning is the Texas Longhorns' starting quarterback and one of college football's most exciting young talents. A member of the legendary Manning football family, Arch has brought electric energy to Austin and has Longhorns fans dreaming big.
